"The Miraculous Kitchen" is a text-based game that is currently being developed. It is about going around your kitchen, gathering various ingredients, and making new types of food of what you can gather. The game is being created in Twine. A preview of the game will be released soon. As soon as the preview of the game will be released, anyone can play it. Right now, pull requests are open so anyone can improve the game.
